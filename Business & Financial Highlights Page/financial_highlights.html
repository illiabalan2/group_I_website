Business & Financial Highlights

Financial plans


Our Business Model

Our company will generate revenue by licensing our technology to smart city developers, selling streetlights to cities, and collecting a membership fee for upkeep and improvements. Additionally, we will earn from selling activity data, collaborating with governments, and running advertisements on smart poles. Each streetlight has a one-time price, and larger projects will incur additional costs in addition to subscription fees for upkeep and repairs. For long-term agreements and large orders, we will provide discounts. We will collaborate with municipal planners, compete on government contracts, and conduct test projects to demonstrate the advantages in order to attract clients. To increase confidence and expand our business, we'll also use events, internet marketing, and providing excellent customer service.

Scalability plans

  • Year 1: Launch the project in a few locations to make sure the AI systems are scalable and efficient. Optimise the embedded system to use less power and save money. Obtain early-stage capital from grants for smart cities or investors.

  • Years 2 - 3: Increase funding to help grow by expanding to other cities. Create remote software updates to gradually enhance AI performance. Make an effort to form strategic alliances with groups that deal with urban planning. Keep up with system updates and maintenance.

  • Years 4 - 5: Try to reach high-density metropolitan regions by expanding worldwide. For steady income, sign long-term agreements with local governments.
