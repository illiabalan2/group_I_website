Market and Customer Page

Understanding our customers and industry landscape.

Overview of Target Market

Our adaptive street lights are designed for key sectors within urban lighting and smart city industries.

  • City Councils & Governments: Cities looking to reduce energy costs and improve public safety.
  • Commercial Properties: Colleges, industrial zones, and residential areas seeking cost-efficient lighting.
  • Urban Developers: Infrastructure projects requiring intelligent lighting solutions.
  • Environmental Organizations: Advocates for eco-friendly urban living and light pollution reduction.

User Personas

  • Urban residents concerned about excessive light pollution affecting health and night visibility.
  • Government sectors looking to cut long-term operational lighting costs.
  • Forward-thinking cities aiming to market themselves as smart, green, and innovative urban environments.

Competitor Comparisons

Our main competitors include Philips, Signify, and Telensa, who offer smart streetlight solutions. However, we stand out due to:

  • AI-Powered Accuracy: Recognizes only humans, vehicles, and bicycles to optimize power usage.
  • Adaptive Brightness: Automatically adjusts to reduce energy waste.
  • Sustainability Focused: Seamlessly integrates with smart cities while cutting down on light pollution.

Why Choose Us?

  • 30%+ energy savings compared to traditional streetlights.
  • Light pollution reduction, creating healthier nighttime environments.
  • Seamless integration with existing infrastructure.
  • Lower long-term maintenance and operational costs.
